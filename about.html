Bailey Island, Maine

Bailey Island is a small island community in Harpswell, Maine, connected to the mainland by the historic Cribstone Bridge. Known for its rugged beauty, working lobster boats, and authentic coastal character, Bailey Island offers a genuine Maine experience.

The island is quiet, unhurried, and perfect for those seeking to escape the noise of everyday life. You'll find rocky shores to explore, local seafood shacks, and stunning views at every turn.
