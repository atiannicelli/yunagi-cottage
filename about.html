About Bailey Island

Bailey Island is a little island community in Harpswell, Maine, connected to the mainland by the Cribstone Bridge. It's got working lobster boats, rocky shores, and that classic Maine vibe.

Not much happens here, which is kind of the point. Good for resetting, exploring the coast, and eating too much seafood.
