Shore Leave

Your escape to the Maine coast

Nestled on the rugged shores of Bailey Island, Maine, Shore Leave offers a rare retreat where time slows, the ocean whispers, and every sunset paints the sky in gold and coral.

This is a place to breathe. To gather with friends and family. To watch the light dance across the water as the day gives way to the calm of evening — when the wind stills and the world feels suspended between day and night.

Whether you're exploring tide pools, enjoying a lobster dinner on the deck, or simply watching the sun sink into the Atlantic, Shore Leave is your home on the coast.
